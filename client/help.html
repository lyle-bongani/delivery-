Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Click the "New Order" button in the top navigation bar, select your delivery address, choose items from our catalog, and proceed to checkout.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 5 minutes of placement, as long as they haven't been accepted by a delivery partner. Use the "Modify Order" option in your active orders.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and current demand. You can see estimated delivery times when placing your order.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes, you can track your delivery in real-time through the "Active Orders" section of your dashboard.

What if my delivery is delayed?

If your delivery is delayed, you'll receive notifications with updates. You can also contact support for assistance.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your profile settings through the user dropdown menu in the top navigation bar.

How can I manage my saved addresses?

Access your saved addresses through the "Addresses" option in your profile menu.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your full card details on our servers.
